Robot who cannot speak

Displays conflicts a Robot who cannot speak deals with other robots who all speak. The Robot who cannot speak was built for war. However, after the war, the robot lost its ability to speak.
It couldn't be fixed by exchanging modules, nor program. The robot was suffering a trauma.

Usual everyday, undermined and discriminated by the robot society, the robot roams around sadly, until the robot encounters a faceless robot who can speek but do not have a face and facial expression. She also lost her face during the war.

A robot who lost his voice, a robot who lost her face. There are 5 more robots to encounter, who also is suffering post-war. The story displays diverse troubles of disabled robots and deep thinking about Human-Like AI and use of them in the military conflicts.

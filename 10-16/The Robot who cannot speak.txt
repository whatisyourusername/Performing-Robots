Robot who cannot speak

It couldn't be fixed by exchanging modules, nor program. The robot was suffering a trauma.

Usual everyday, undermined and discriminated by the robot society, the robot roams around sadly, until the robot encounters a faceless robot who can speek but do not have a face and facial expression.
